Pickleball Gear Guide is an independent site written for recreational and intermediate pickleball players who want straightforward, practical advice on paddles, shoes, apparel, and accessories.

We're not affiliated with any paddle brand, league, or manufacturer. Our goal is simple: help you avoid overspending on gear that doesn't fit your game, and point you toward products that genuinely suit your skill level and budget.

We research real products, real approximate prices, and real buyer considerations before writing anything. When we're not confident about a specific spec or price, we say so and point you to double-check the current listing.

This site earns commissions from qualifying purchases made through affiliate links. See our [affiliate disclosure](/disclosure/) for full details.
